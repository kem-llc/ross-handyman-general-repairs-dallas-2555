Our story

Dallas’s Go-To Handyman Since 2009

To provide Dallas homeowners with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make life easier and homes better.

Ross Handyman & General Repairs started as a small operation serving neighbors in the Ross Avenue area. With a focus on honest work and fair prices, we quickly built a reputation for reliability. Over the years, we’ve expanded our skills to cover plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and more.

Today, we serve the entire Dallas metro with a team of experienced professionals. We take pride in every job, big or small, and treat your home with the same respect we’d give our own.
